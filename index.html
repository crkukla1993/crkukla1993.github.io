//About Me:

My name is Christopher Kukla. I have always like building and creating things; LEGOs were my favorite toy. During high school, I had taken multiple computer aided drafting courses. These courses got me interested in Engineering. I took that interest to Northern Illinois University, enrolling in their Mechanical Engineering program. Over time, I realized that engineering wasn't satisfying my passion to create. So, I left NIU, and went on to study at Illinois State University majoring in Computer Science.

My education has taught me a lot and so has my internship experience. I spent two summers and one winter break working as an intern at the Village of Palatine, Illinois. During my internship I taught myself C#, ASP.NET, Javascript, and JQuery. I needed these languages to build the systems that the Village still has in place today.

There is more to me than just my education and my experience. I have a passion for development. I enjoy the time I spend programming. I want to create a good user experience. I want my work to show that I love, and care about what I do. The passion I have is what drives me. I hope that my passion can drive your business.
